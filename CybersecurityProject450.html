What is Cybersecurity?


Cybersecurity is all the protection called that keeps a computer, laptop, smartphone, and tablet from someone stealing important and private information.

What are Cybersecurity risk?


Cybersecurity risks are the weaknesses (or vulnerabilities) that may lead to someone stealing important information.
For example, a risk could be weak passwords, coming in contact with malware, or being part of an insecure network.

How to file a complaint?

It is important to report any cybersecurity issues. The best way to report a crime is to contact reliable and trust worth sources, such as the FBI website.
It is a great source to stay up to date on scams, attacks, threats but also to file a complaint.


If you think you fell victim to a crime file a report with the FBI.
